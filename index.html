About Efebia

Efebia was an institution in ancient Athens where young men at the age of 18 entered in conscription lists and received military, literaly and musical education. According to this philosophy, we founded Efebia at the end of 2018. Our employees are 'efebi': they all can grow in a safe environment, developing his or her own ideas and projects. Their education and our continuous improvement are based on training on the job and research projects. Learning a new programming language, developing complex software architectures, imaging new products and services: all of this is accomplished in synergy with the feedback of our customers, who rely on us to fulfill their ambitions.
Efebia is not just a company, but first of all it's a big family where we grow together.
